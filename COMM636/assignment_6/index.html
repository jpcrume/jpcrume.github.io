About Suzanne Collins

Suzanne Collins is a renowned American author, best known for The Hunger Games series, a global phenomenon exploring themes of survival, rebellion, and justice. She also authored The Underland Chronicles, a fantasy-adventure series set in a hidden world beneath New York City. Before becoming a bestselling novelist, Collins wrote for children's television, which shaped her talent for crafting compelling, emotionally rich stories.
