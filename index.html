Artificial Intelligence in Special Education

A new development within the classroom...

Artificial Intelligence, otherwise known as AI, is a hot topic in the technological field. One recent thing I have learned about and been fascinated with is the idea of artificial intelligence being used within the classroom (especially for children with disabilities both learning and physical). What first intrigued me with this topic is that my older brother has multiple learning disabilites and he loves technology. Had more advanced technology been involved in his K-12 Education I'm sure he would have loved it and been more excited and encouraged to learn. The first article I found about it can be accessed by clicking here! This specific article written by the Tech Advocate describes how the AI can present material to students who struggle with readining advanced texts, it can also help educators identify and discover if a child may present signs of a learning disability. AI can also help students get more consistent and reliable feedback. One of the most highlighted aspects of AI in the classroom is how it can collect useful data for teachers.
